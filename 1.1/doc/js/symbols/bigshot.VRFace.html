Class Detail
bigshot.VRFace(owner, key, topLeft_, width_, u, v, onLoaded)
Creates a new VR cube face.
Parameters:
{bigshot.VRPanorama} owner
the VR panorama this face is part of.
{String} key
the identifier for the face. "f" is front, "b" is back, "u" is up, "d" is down, "l" is left and "r" is right.
{point} topLeft_
the top-left corner of the quad.
{number} width_
the length of the sides of the face, expressed in multiples of u and v.
{vector} u
basis vector going from the top left corner along the top edge of the face
{vector} v
basis vector going from the top left corner along the left edge of the face
